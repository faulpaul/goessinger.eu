About Me

Hi, my name is Paul and I work as head of eBusiness at BayWa AG. My main focus is on innovation and digitalization. Let's get the company ready to tackle the next 95+ years!

Previously I worked as a corporate strategy project manager and compliance manager after many years in different IT positions (“manager UNIX systems” @ DAB Bank AG, “IT consultant” @ Red Hat).

I have a master’s degree (MBA) from the Munich Business School in "Business Administration" and a magister in political science from the Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität in Munich. I finished my Abitur (~A-Levels) at Gymnasium Tutzing.

I am also an independent local councilor in my home town Bernried for an independent electoral list. See ÜFW (Überparteiliche Freie Wähler) for more information.

In my spare time I like to backpack through far away countries, watch birds, hiking in the mountains or spend time on coursera to learn new things.
